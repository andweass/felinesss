ABOUT

"feline" is a sanctuary for cats, dedicated to offering a secure and cozy space for stray felines. Upon arrival, all cats available for adoption will undergo spaying or neutering. Having endured hardships living independently on the streets, these beloved furry companions are ensured a loving adoption into forever homes by the caring individuals at "feline."
